Shipment Policy

01

Shipping Services

Tapman Cold Storage provides transport services for stored goods based on prior agreements. All shipments are subject to availability and scheduling as per the terms of the service agreement.

02

Delivery Times

Delivery timelines will be mutually agreed upon during the service arrangement. Tapman Cold Storage strives to meet delivery deadlines but is not liable for delays due to unforeseen circumstances.

03

Shipping Costs

Shipping fees are determined based on factors like distance, storage duration, and weight. Detailed fee structures will be provided in the client agreement.

04

Risk and Responsibility

Once goods leave Tapman Cold Storage premises, liability for those goods is transferred to the carrier or the receiving party, as applicable under the terms of the shipping contract.
